SMS Terms

Text-message terms for service requests.

Transactional SMS terms for Murfreesboro Home Pros service-request updates and one-partner-at-a-time routing.

Transactional SMS program

Murfreesboro Home Pros may send transactional text messages about an active service request when you provide SMS consent or initiate SMS with us. Messages may include request confirmation, missing-information questions, one-partner-at-a-time routing updates, missed-call follow-up, appointment coordination, and feedback requests.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Separate consent is required for promotional messages.

One-partner routing scope

When you consent, you authorize us to share the details of your request with one selected local service partner at a time for the purpose of coordinating your request. We do not sell your request to a vendor list.
