Privacy Notice

How homeowner request information is handled.

Privacy notice for Murfreesboro Home Pros intake, matching, routing, CRM, and SMS records.

What we collect

For service requests, Murfreesboro Home Pros may collect your name, mobile phone, optional email, project/problem details, urgency, project stage, ZIP/neighborhood/location context, consent records, and communication metadata needed to coordinate the request.

How we use it

We use request information to qualify the request, coordinate one selected local service partner at a time, send transactional updates when consented, maintain compliance records, improve matching quality, and operate the service.

Boundaries

We do not sell, rent, auction, append, or redistribute homeowner request data beyond the one-partner routing purpose without fresh, specific approval. Providers are independent businesses responsible for their own inspections, advice, estimates, contracts, permits, work, warranties, and outcomes.

Status

This is a launch placeholder pending final legal review. It exists so the consent and CRM/SMS architecture have a visible policy target before production intake is enabled.
