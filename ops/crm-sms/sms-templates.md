# SMS Templates

All outbound messages require prior consent where applicable. Homeowner messages must support STOP/HELP handling through the SMS provider.

## Homeowner: intake received

Hi {{first_name}}, this is Murfreesboro Home Pros. We got your {{project_category}} request for {{location_text}}. We’ll review the details and work on a one-provider-at-a-time match. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Homeowner: need more info

Quick question so we route this correctly: {{question}} Reply here when you can. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Provider: dispatch / claim request

MHP lead: {{project_category}} / {{timing}} / {{location_text}}. Problem: {{problem}}. Stage: {{project_stage}}. Reply ACCEPT to claim, DECLINE if not a fit.

## Provider: accepted acknowledgement

Thanks — marked accepted. Please contact {{homeowner_name}} at {{mobile_phone}}. Keep us posted when contact/estimate is scheduled.

## Homeowner: provider accepted

Good news — we have a provider reviewing your request. They should contact you soon. If anything changes, reply here. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Homeowner: follow-up

Checking in from Murfreesboro Home Pros: did the provider contact you about your {{project_category}} request? Reply YES, NO, or NEED HELP. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Homeowner: feedback request

How did the referral go? Reply 1-5 for your experience, or add a quick note. This helps us improve provider matching. Reply STOP to opt out.

## HELP response

Murfreesboro Home Pros helps homeowners get matched with local service providers one at a time. Reply STOP to opt out, or reply with your question.
